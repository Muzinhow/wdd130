Our Most Popular Rafting Trips

Colorado River | Full Day Adventure

Enjoy an unforgettable full-day rafting experience through the legendary Colorado River. With Class III and IV rapids, this trip blends adrenaline with breathtaking canyon views. Perfect for adventure seekers who want the ultimate rafting challenge.

Pacuare River | Jungle Expedition

Travel deep into Costa Rica’s lush rainforest while conquering the Pacuare’s famous rapids. This trip includes scenic waterfalls, wildlife sightings, and iconic turquoise waters. Ideal for those wanting both thrills and nature immersion.

Zambezi River | Extreme Rapids

Considered one of the world's wildest rafting experiences, the Zambezi offers massive Class V rapids near Victoria Falls. This adventure is intense, powerful, and perfect for experienced rafters wanting to push their limits.
